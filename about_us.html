About Us


Article + Integrity = Artegrity
Easiest Way to Analyze the News



At Artegrity, we come together to attempt to solve one of the biggest problems in our current society: Fake news. News these days is prone to bias and written in such a way to elicit emotions from specific audiences. Readers are then caught in a scenario where all news they read is either biased or written with unsaid motives; how will they know what is real or fake?

That's where we come in. By producing an easy and fast way to objectively analyze and evaluate the news, news readers should feel confident in taking any news article they see and check for the validity and integrity of that article. Readers will also be able to see trending articles with their corresponding grade, and will be able to share these article reports with anyone. Fighting fake news is a difficult task, but Artegrity is leading this battle battle one article at a time.
